Steps to play the Game:


  • Click on the checkers to get the cordinates of checkers piece.
  • Open console using shift+ ctrl + j
  • Call firstMove(x,y) to begin the game. (x,y) can be get by clicking the checkers you want to move.
  • After then keep calling the move(from_X,from_Y,to_X,to_Y)
Note:
  • To win the game you should be left with 1 checkers only.
  • You can move the checkers in diagonal direction and in linear Left and Right Direction
  • To make a move, move the checkers the checkers over the checkers towards available spot.
